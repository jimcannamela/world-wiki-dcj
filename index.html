The World of New Xanthe

New Xanthe is the capital city of the United Confederation of New Xanthe. A real world that is part of a future reality, where humans have colonized the red planet, Mars. The colony is the brainchild of tech entrepreneur Elon Musk, who, through his company SpaceX, has developed the technology to transport humans and supplies to Mars. Musk conducted his first trip to the colony utilizing his Mars-bound spacecraft "Heart of Gold". The colony was founded in the year 2056 and is situated in the Xanthe Terra region of Mars, which is known for its ancient riverbeds and canyons.
